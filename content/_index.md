### Welcome to the local development environment for TCS Labs Academy

> This page only shows during local preview (`make site`). When published to the
> [Meshery Academy](https://cloud.meshery.io/academy) platform, the Academy renders two main
> sections — **Learning Paths** and **Challenges** — plus **Certifications**.

#### Build workflow

> 1. **Develop locally** — author Markdown under `content/`, preview with `make site`.
> 2. **Open a pull request** — content stays private to your organization until you publish.
> 3. **Publish when ready** — release it to your organization on the Academy platform.

**Need help?** See the [README](https://github.com/meshery-extensions/tcslabs-academy/blob/master/README.md)
and the [Curriculum outline](https://github.com/meshery-extensions/tcslabs-academy/blob/master/CURRICULUM.md).
